Campus Life

school's assembly hall

was built in 1999

Hiranandani Foundation School's assembly hall is used almost daily, mainly for assemblies and announcing rewards. In almost every assembly, our principal, Neelu Lamba comes.



school library

was built in 2003

Our school library is small but has more than 500 books in it. We have only 1 period of library in a week. Students of 9th and 10th come to the library to study if the period is proxy.
